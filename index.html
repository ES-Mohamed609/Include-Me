About Include Me

Empowering individuals with disabilities to overcome employment challenges

Our Mission

Include Me is a comprehensive digital empowerment platform designed to help individuals with disabilities overcome employment challenges and achieve their career goals. We provide accessible job search tools, intelligent matching algorithms, personalized learning pathways, and a supportive community to ensure everyone has equal opportunities in the workforce.

Powered by Huawei Cloud

Our platform leverages cutting-edge technology to deliver efficient, secure, and accessible services:

  • Huawei Cloud Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade cloud computing ensuring 99.9% uptime, scalability, and low operational costs
  • AI-Powered Matching: Advanced algorithms analyze CVs and job requirements to provide intelligent job matching with accuracy scores
  • Accessibility First: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant design with screen reader support, keyboard navigation, and customizable display options
  • Data Security: End-to-end encryption and compliance with GDPR and data protection regulations
  • Real-time Processing: Instant CV analysis and job matching powered by cloud computing
